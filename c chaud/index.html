About

Characters like Mickey Mouse have become iconic symbols of entertainment and nostalgia. As these characters enter the public domain, there is an opportunity to preserve their legacy through blockchain technology. $mickey on Base is a memecoin (unaffiliated with Disney) that embodies this vision by placing these characters on the blockchain, allowing for new forms of immutable interaction and engagement.

Tokenomics

Token Overview

The $MICKEY token is a community-driven memecoin that aims to celebrate and preserve the legacy of beloved characters. It is built on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring transparency and security.
